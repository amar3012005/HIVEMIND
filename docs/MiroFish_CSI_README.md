# MiroFish_CSI

**MiroFish_CSI** is the CSI-optimized research and swarm-intelligence direction for HIVEMIND and Deep Research.

It combines:
- persistent shared cognition,
- structured trails,
- blueprint reuse,
- stigmergic coordination,
- report provenance,
- and durable memory promotion.

The goal is not just to answer a question. The goal is to make the system remember, coordinate, improve, and compound over time.

## What MiroFish_CSI Is

MiroFish_CSI is the research-oriented operating mode of the system:

- **Deep Research** gathers evidence, extracts claims, verifies contradictions, and synthesizes reports.
- **CSI** turns those outputs into a persistent cognitive environment.
- **TrailStore** preserves execution history, provenance, and reusable paths.
- **Blueprints** capture repeated success as reusable procedure.
- **Promoted memory** stores high-value findings so future runs can reuse them.

If MiroFish emphasizes simulation and social interaction, MiroFish_CSI emphasizes:
- traceability,
- knowledge compounding,
- controlled reuse,
- and research-grade provenance.

## Core Thesis

> Persistent shared cognition can outperform isolated agent reasoning when memory, behavior, routing, and procedural learning are externalized into a structured environment that agents continuously mutate, query, and inherit from.

This means:
- intelligence lives in the environment, not only in the agent,
- repeated success becomes procedure,
- behavior is traceable,
- and future runs can reuse durable knowledge instead of starting from zero.

## Agent Roles

The current CSI-oriented Deep Research flow uses three canonical roles:

- **Faraday**: discovers sources and observations.
- **Feynmann**: extracts claims and reasoning structure.
- **Turing**: verifies, challenges, and resolves contradictions.

Report synthesis is a final stage, not a fourth research agent.

## What Makes It Different From Standard Deep Research

Standard research workflows usually:
- search,
- summarize,
- report,
- and discard most of the process.

MiroFish_CSI instead:
- records the path taken,
- stores the best paths as blueprints,
- promotes important findings into memory,
- reuses retained memory in future runs,
- and keeps a golden provenance line for every report.

MiroFish_CSI is best when you need:

- and reusable procedural intelligence.

## What It Is Not

It is not:
- a pure simulation engine,
- a generic chat wrapper,
- a stateless RAG pipeline,
- or a throwaway report generator.

## Recommended Operating Flow

1. Seed the question.
2. Gather sources and observations.
3. Extract claims and check contradictions.
4. Pause before final synthesis.
5. Recall from retained memory and blueprints.
6. Generate a sectioned report.
7. Promote the strongest findings to memory.
8. Write the golden provenance line.
9. Reuse that retained knowledge in the next run.

## Why This Matters

This turns Deep Research into a compounding research workspace:
- better future recall,
- better blueprint reuse,
- better report quality,
- and stronger evidence continuity across sessions.
